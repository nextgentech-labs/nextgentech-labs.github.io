Built by Practitioners,
Not Professors

Our founders have worked inside the industry, taught inside institutions, and understand the gap firsthand. That is what makes this different.

"The world does not need more degrees.
It needs people who can actually do the work.
We are here to make that happen — at scale."

NextGen Tech Labs was founded on a single belief: that the gap between what colleges teach and what companies need is not a small problem — it is a crisis. We exist to fix it by bringing the people who live and breathe this work directly into classrooms. Our vision is not bound by any region or geography. It is global — every institution, every student, every skill gap that stands between potential and opportunity.
